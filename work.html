Billing Platform for Canadian Immigration Consultants

crisis_alert The Problem

Canadian immigration consultants needed a billing system that could handle multiple payment processors (credit cards, pre-authorized debits, e-Transfer) while maintaining strict compliance with trust accounting rules, PIPEDA privacy laws, and GST/HST regulations.

lightbulb The Solution

I architected a multi-processor payment system with an abstraction layer that unified Stripe (credit cards), Rotessa (PAD), and Interac (e-Transfer) behind a single interface. Built-in trust accounting ensures compliance, while webhook reconciliation handles the complexity of multiple payment sources.
